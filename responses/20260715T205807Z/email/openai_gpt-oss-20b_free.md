The council’s confirmation of your garden’s status, along with a letter of support, will be required as part of the application.

**2. Minimum and maximum grant amounts**  
The CRG offers grants ranging from $5,000 to $30,000, depending on the scope and impact of the project. The exact amount you can request will be negotiated during the application review, but based on your projected costs for rainwater tanks and shade structure, a request within this range would be appropriate. We do not impose a hard minimum; however, applications for amounts below $5,000 are typically considered too small for the CRG and may be redirected to other funding streams.

**3. Eligible infrastructure costs**  
Infrastructure that directly enhances community resilience to climate change is fully eligible. Installation of rainwater tanks and a shade structure that improves the garden’s operational capacity during hotter weather falls squarely within our eligible cost categories. You should detail the cost of the tanks, shade structure, installation labor, and any associated compliance or safety works (e.g., waterproofing, structural assessment) in your budget.
