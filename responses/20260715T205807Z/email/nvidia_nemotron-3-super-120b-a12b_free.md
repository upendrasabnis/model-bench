**Auspiced groups**

The council must also agree to receive the grant funds on your behalf and to acquit them in accordance with the program’s reporting schedule.

**Grant size**  
The Community Resilience Grant operates within a band of **$5,000 minimum** and **$50,000 maximum** per successful applicant. There is no prescribed “average” award; the amount is determined by the scope and demonstrated need of the proposed activities. Your planned rainwater‑tank and shade‑structure installation would typically fall well within this range, especially if you obtain competitive quotes and show a clear cost‑benefit analysis.

**Eligible costs**  
Capital infrastructure that directly enhances the resilience of a community‑based activity is an eligible expense. This includes the purchase and installation of rainwater harvesting tanks, associated plumbing, and shade structures (e.g., pergolas, shade sails, or shade‑cloth systems) that protect volunteers, visitors, and produce from extreme heat. Operational costs such as staff wages, ongoing maintenance, or consumables are **not** covered under this grant round.
