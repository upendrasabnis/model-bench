## 4.  Assessment & Scoring: From Subjective to Data‑Driven  

### 4.1  The Review Process Reimagined  

| Aspect | Manual Review | GMS‑Enabled Review |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Reviewers** | 5–10 per application | 20+ reviewers, remote or in‑person |
| **Scoring Rubric** | Paper forms | Digital rubric, auto‑calculation |
| **Bias Mitigation** | Hard to control | Blind review options, weighting |
| **Consensus Building** | Manual meeting notes | Real‑time voting, discussion threads |
| **Speed** | 2–3 weeks | 48‑72 hours |

#### Case Study: *Health Equity Initiative*  
- **Problem**: Inconsistent scoring across panels; required 5 rounds of re‑examination.  
- **Solution**: Adopted a GMS that enforced a **standard rubric** and a **blinded scoring interface**.  
- **Outcome**: Scoring variance dropped by 42 %; final decisions made 36 % faster.
