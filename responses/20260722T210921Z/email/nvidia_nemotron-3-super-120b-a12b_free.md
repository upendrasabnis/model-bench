The CRG program expressly permits applications from unincorporated community organisations that are auspiced by an eligible local government entity. The council must also confirm that it holds the necessary public liability and volunteer insurance coverage, which you indicated is already in place.

**Grant size**  
For the current funding round, the minimum award is $5,000 and the maximum award is $50,000 (GST exclusive). Applications requesting amounts outside this range will be deemed ineligible. Given your project scope, a request in the $15,000–$30,000 band would be typical, but you are free to propose any amount within the limits that reflects the true cost of the rainwater tanks and shade structure.

**Eligibility of infrastructure costs**  
Capital works such as the installation of rainwater harvesting tanks and permanent shade structures are considered eligible expenditures under the “infrastructure resilience” category of the CRG. The grant can cover the purchase of materials, contractor labour, and associated design or engineering fees. Ongoing operational costs (e.g., water utilities, routine maintenance) are not eligible, but you may include a modest contingency (up to 10 % of the total budget) for unforeseen site‑specific expenses.
