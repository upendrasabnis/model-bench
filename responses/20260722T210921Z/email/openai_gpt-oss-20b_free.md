Thank you for reaching out and for your interest in the Community Resilience Grant round. I’m happy to clarify the eligibility criteria and the documentation you’ll need to prepare for a full application.

**1. Eligibility of unincorporated, council‑auspiced groups**  
The Grant is open to community‑run groups, including those that are not formally registered as charities or incorporated entities. The key requirement is that the group is *sponsored* by a statutory body that can assume responsibility for governance, compliance and insurance. Because your garden operates under the auspices of the local councilizer, you meet this criterion. The council must provide a written sponsorship letter confirming its support, stating that it will be responsible for the project’s insurance and compliance with all applicable regulations. This letter should be dated and signed by an elected council member or the council’s chief executive.

**2. Minimum/maximum grant amount**  
There is no fixed minimum or maximum funding threshold for the Community Resilience Grant. However, the funding range typically falls between **$5,000 and $20,000** per project, depending on the scope and impact of the proposed activity. A detailed budget will help us determine the appropriate award level. If your estimated cost for the rainwater tanks and shade structure falls within this range, you will likely be considered for full funding of that portion of the project.

**3. Eligibility of infrastructure costs**  
Infrastructure that directly contributes to community resilience—such as rainwater harvesting systems, shade structures, or other climate‑adaptation measures—is fully eligible. The grant specifically supports projects that enhance the ability of local communities to withstand extreme weather and other environmental risks. Ensure that your budget clearly links each line item to resilience outcomes (e.g., reduced water consumption, extended growing season, heat mitigation for volunteers).

**4. Supporting documents you should prepare**  
To streamline the review process, please gather the following:

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Council sponsorship letter** | Confirms council’s responsibility and insurance coverage. |
| **Proof of council insurance** | Policy numbers and coverage details. |
| **Project budget** | Detailed cost breakdown (materials, labour, contingency). |
| **Project timeline** | Milestones and completion dates. |
| **Risk assessment** | Identification of potential hazards and mitigation strategies. |
| **Financial statements** | Recent turnover figures and any existing funding sources. |
| **Community benefit statement** | Explanation of how the garden and its infrastructure serve the local community. |
| **Letters of support** (optional) | From local businesses, schools, or community members endorsing the project. |
