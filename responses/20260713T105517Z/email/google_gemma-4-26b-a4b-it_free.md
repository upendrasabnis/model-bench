**1. Scope of the Audit**
The post-award audit is primarily a financial audit designed to ensure that expenditures align with the approved budget lines and that all funds were used for their intended purposes. However, while the auditor does not need to perform a technical engineering assessment, they will need to verify that the financial expenditures correspond to the milestones reported in your technical documentation. For example, if a significant portion of the budget was allocated to hardware procurement, the auditor will cross-reference these costs against the technical progress reported in your quarterly updates to ensure the project's physical progress matches the financial spend.

**2. Auditor Qualifications**
To ensure the integrity of the audit, the independent auditor must be a firm accredited by a recognized professional body. Given the regulatory framework of this grant, we require the auditor to be a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales (ICAEW) or the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA). While we respect the standards of CPA-qualified firms, for this specific grant, the auditor must adhere to UK-specific auditing standards to satisfy our internal compliance protocols.

**3. Timing and Submission**
Regarding the 90-day submission window, the "clock" commences on the date of the actual completion of the final project activity, as defined in your project timeline. The submission of the final technical report is a separate milestone; however, we recommend that you aim to have your audit finalized as close to the project completion date as possible to avoid any administrative delays in the final disbursement.

**4. Documentation Package**
A summary of key line items will not be sufficient for a full compliance audit. You must be prepared to provide a comprehensive documentation package. This includes, but is not limited to, detailed payroll records for project staff, all procurement invoices, receipts, and fully executed sub-contractor agreements. Maintaining a rigorous "audit trail" throughout the implementation phase will be vital to a smooth year-end process.

**5. Electronic versus Hard-Copy Delivery**
We have transitioned to a fully digital compliance workflow. The audited financial statements and the accompanying auditor's report should be uploaded directly to the grant management portal. We do not require hard-copy, mailed versions, provided the electronic upload is a high-resolution, signed PDF.
