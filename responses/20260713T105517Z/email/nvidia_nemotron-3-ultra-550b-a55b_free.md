Thank you for your detailed inquiry and for the important work Green Horizons Initiative is undertaking in the Eastside district. We appreciate the opportunity to provide clarity on the post‑award audit requirements for grant CRE‑2025‑12. Below are responses to each of your questions.

1. Scope of the audit – The audit encompasses both a financial review of expenditures against the approved budget and a verification of the technical performance metrics documented in your quarterly reports. The auditor should confirm that kilowatt‑hours generated, system uptime, and other key performance indicators align with the targets set forth in the award agreement and that reported outcomes are substantiated by operational data.

2. Auditor qualifications – The independent auditor must be a firm accredited by a recognized UK professional body such as ICAEW, ACCA, or ICAS. A CPA‑qualified firm based outside the UK will be accepted only if it holds a current practicing certificate from one of the aforementioned UK bodies or can demonstrate equivalence through a formal mutual‑recognition agreement. Independence criteria as defined by the FRC Ethical Standard must be met in all cases.

3. Timing and submission – The 90‑day period commences on the official grant completion date, which is the date the last eligible project activity is completed and confirmed by the programme officer. Submission of the final technical report does not alter this start date; however, we encourage you to submit the technical report promptly so that the audit can proceed without delay.

4. Documentation package – In addition to the audited financial statements, the funder requires a complete supporting schedule that includes payroll records, procurement invoices, sub‑contractor agreements, and a reconciliation of budgeted versus actual line items. A summary of key line items alone will not satisfy the compliance review.

5. Electronic versus hard‑copy delivery – The audit report and all supporting schedules must be uploaded electronically through the grant management portal in PDF/A format. A printed, signed copy is not required unless specifically requested during a spot‑check review.

Please ensure that your selected audit firm is engaged early enough to meet the 90‑day deadline. Should you need further assistance or wish to confirm the eligibility of a specific audit firm, do not hesitate to contact me directly.
