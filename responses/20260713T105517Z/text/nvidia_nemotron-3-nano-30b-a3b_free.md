## 7. Challenges & Practical Mitigation Strategies  

### 7.1. Copyright & Intellectual‑Property Clearance  
- **Pre‑emptive audit:** Create an inventory of rights‑holders; use “orphan‑work” procedures where applicable.  
- **Licensing models:** Apply Creative Commons licences (CC‑BY‑SA) for public‑domain material; negotiate image‑licence agreements for commercial use.  

### 7.2. Technical Infrastructure & Skills Gaps  
- **Phased rollout:** Start with pilot scans to test workflow before scaling.  
- **Partnerships:** Collaborate with universities or tech incubators for access to 3‑D scanning labs.  
- **Training:** Allocate budget for staff upskilling (e.g., “Digital Preservation Certificate”).  

### 7.3. Sustainability After the Grant  
- **Revenue streams:** Offer paid virtual tours, sell high‑resolution prints, licence content to educational platforms.  
- **Maintenance fund:** Set aside 5‑10 % of total budget for ongoing hosting and updates.  
- **Community ownership:** Transfer governance of digital assets to a stewardship board after project completion.
