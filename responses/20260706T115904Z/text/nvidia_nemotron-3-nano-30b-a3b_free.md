10.2. Q: Do I need to match funds?  

165  
 A: Some programs require a cash or in‑kind match; check the specific terms of the call.  

166  
10.3. Q: How do I ensure data collected is culturally appropriate?  

167  
 A: Engage community elders in data‑collection design and obtain informed consent using culturally relevant processes.  

168  
10.4. Q: What if my community lacks internet connectivity for reporting?  

169  
 A: Use offline data collection tools (e.g., paper forms) and upload aggregated results when connectivity is restored.

171  
 A: Many funders allow external experts if they are essential to capacity building and are priced competitively.
