**Eligible costs**  
Capital infrastructure that directly enhances community resilience to climate‑related stresses is an eligible expense. This includes the purchase and installation of rainwater harvesting systems, shade structures, and associated fittings (e.g., gutters, pumps, foundations). Operational costs such as staff salaries, ongoing maintenance, or routine consumables are not eligible unless they are demonstrably linked to the delivery of the grant‑funded activity and are clearly itemised in the budget.
