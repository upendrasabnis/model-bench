While an unincorporated association is not a separate legal entity, it may still be eligible when it operates “under the auspices of” a council, as long as the council’s insurance and governance frameworks cover the group’s activities. We will need a signed letter from the council confirming this relationship and outlining the scope of coverage.

   The current funding round allocates grants ranging from **$5,000 to $25,000**. Your proposed investment in rain‑water tanks and a shade structure falls comfortably within this band; however, the final award amount will be determined by the overall budget available and the relative priority of each applicant.

   Infrastructure that directly enhances the community’s capacity to withstand climate‑related stressors is an eligible cost category. This includes, but is not limited to, water‑capture systems, shading structures, solar panels, and resilient building modifications. The key requirement is that the expense must be **tangible, capital‑cost‑oriented, and directly tied to resilience outcomes**. Therefore, the rain‑water tanks and shade structure you plan to install qualify as eligible infrastructure.
