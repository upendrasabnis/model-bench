For this round, the grant offers a minimum award of $1,000 and a maximum award of $15,000. The amount you request will be reviewed against the project’s cost, the impact proposed, and the overall competitiveness of the application pool. **3. Eligibility of infrastructure costs**  
Infrastructure items that enhance the resilience and sustainability of a community garden are explicitly included in the eligible cost list. Rainwater tanks, shade structures, upgraded irrigation systems, and other climate‑adaptation measures are all eligible. We do, however, require that the costs be directly linked to the resilience objectives of the grant (e.g., reducing water consumption, extending the operating SQLite to hot months, safeguarding produce). Please ensure that each line item in your budget is supported by a quotation and a clear statement of its resilience benefit.
