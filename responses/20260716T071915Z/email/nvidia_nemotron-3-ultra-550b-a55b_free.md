Thank you for your enquiry regarding the Community Resilience Grant round. I appreciate you reaching out to confirm eligibility before investing time in a full application, and I am pleased to address each of your questions in detail.

**Auspicing Arrangements:** Unincorporated groups operating under a formal auspicing agreement with a local government authority are eligible to apply, provided the auspicing organisation (in this case, your local council) meets our standard eligibility criteria—specifically, that it is a legal entity with an ABN, holds appropriate public liability insurance, and agrees to accept legal and financial responsibility for the grant. You will need to upload a signed auspicing agreement or letter of support from the council confirming these arrangements at the application stage.

**Grant Amounts:** For this round, the minimum request is $5,000 and the maximum is $50,000. Your proposed project budget should fall within this range. Please note that we cannot fund 100 per cent of project costs; a minimum 20 per cent contribution (cash or in-kind) from your group or the auspicing body is required.

**Eligible Costs:** Capital infrastructure that directly enhances community resilience—such as rainwater harvesting systems and permanent shade structures—is considered an eligible expenditure. Costs must be directly attributable to the project, supported by written quotes, and incurred after the funding agreement is executed. Operational costs (e.g., ongoing water bills) and retrospective expenditure are not eligible.

**Supporting Documentation:** In addition to the auspicing agreement, please prepare:
- A project plan with timeline, risk assessment, and maintenance strategy
- Two independent written quotes for each major infrastructure item
- Evidence of land tenure or council permission for the installation site
- Your group’s most recent financial statement (showing the ~$40,000 turnover)
- A brief governance statement outlining your committee structure and volunteer management
- Letters of community support (minimum two) demonstrating broader benefit

Applications close at 5:00 pm AEST on the date advertised on our website. I encourage you to begin drafting your application in our online portal early, as the system allows you to save and return.
