**1. Eligibility of unincorporated groups under council auspices**  
The CRG is open to “unincorporated community groups” that are operating under the auspices of a local council or other statutory body, even if the group has not yet formalised as a registered charity or incorporated entity. The key requirement is that the council must provide a written statement of support confirming that it is acting as the statutory representative for the group, that it holds all necessary insurances, and that it has reviewed the proposed project. Once you have this council‐sponsored letter, the group will be considered an eligible applicant.

**2. Grant amount limits**  
The CRG offers a tiered funding range:  
- **Small projects**: $1,000 – $5,000  
- **Medium projects**: $5,001 – $15,000  
- **Large projects**: $15,001 – $30,000  

Your proposed installation of rainwater tanks and a shade structure falls comfortably within the medium tier; we can support up to $15,000, subject to the overall budget and justification provided in the application. Please note that the grant is a single‑stage award; it will be paid in two instalments (50 % upon approval, 50 % upon completion and audit).

**3. Eligible infrastructure costs**
