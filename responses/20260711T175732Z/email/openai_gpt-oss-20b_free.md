**1. Eligibility of unincorporated groups auspiced by a council**  
The Community Resilience Grant is open to any community‑based organisation that can demonstrate a clear link to local resilience and sustainable development.ش تضمين، وهي تتلقى الدعم الآلي من المجلس المحلي، يُعتبر مشروعكم مؤهلاً. We require evidence that the council holds the necessary insurances and that the project will be managed under its oversight. In practice, this means you’ll need a formal letter of support from the council confirming their auspices, as well as proof of the council’s insurance coverage (e.g., a copy of the policy or a written statement).

**2. Minimum and maximum grant amounts**  
The grant pool for this round is $200 000, allocated in increments of $5 000 to $20 000, depending on the scope of the proposal. For projects of the size you described (rainwater tanks and a shade structure), the typical award range is $5 presta $10 000. Please note that the final amount will be negotiated based on cost breakdowns, projected community benefit, and the funding balance at the time of award.

**3. Eligible costs – infrastructure**  
Infrastructure upgrades that directly enhance resilience are fully eligible. This includes the purchase and installation of rainwater tanks, shade sails, and associated fittings, provided the costs can be substantiated with supplier quotes and installation contracts. We also cover ancillary costs such as design fees, engineering assessments, and compliance inspections, up to a maximum of 20 % of the total project cost.
